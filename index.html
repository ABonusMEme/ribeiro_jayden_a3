My Skills

Hi! I’m Jayden Ribeiro, a 19-year-old creative from Toronto, Canada. I thrive at the intersection of design, innovation, and storytelling, combining my skills and passions to create meaningful, user-focused projects. Whether I’m designing, brainstorming solutions, or capturing the world through my camera lens, I’m driven by curiosity and a deep appreciation for impactful ideas. My journey is fueled by a commitment to learning and a love for crafting experiences that resonate with people on a personal level. The *Elements* rolling papers rebrand is one of the projects I’m most proud of. I was tasked with breathing new life into an iconic product and came up with a new identity that struck a balance between the product’s traditional roots and a contemporary aesthetic. I worked on logo redesigns, cohesive packaging concepts, and I enhanced user appeal while keeping the brand true to its essence. As a deep dive into branding and storytelling, it was a chance to bring together creativity and strategic thinking to create a design that resonates with today’s audiences. One of my highlights of work was developing a customer profile for a blockchain health records system. The aim was to reframe healthcare data management around the needs of people living in dense urban areas. I took a humanizing approach to the technology by creating a user narrative that focused on security, accessibility and convenience. This project helped me to strengthen my ability to connect emerging tech with real world applications and also taught me the value of empathy in UX design. I want to leave a lasting impact whether it’s through design, photography or crafting innovative solutions. If you’re interested in working together or want to learn more about my work, please don’t hesitate to get in touch; I’m always happy to connect and chat about what we do!
